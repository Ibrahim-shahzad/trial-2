Spain



1. Location and Geography

Spain is a country in Southern Europe, occupying most of the Iberian Peninsula, which it shares with Portugal. It also includes the Balearic Islands (in the Mediterranean), the Canary Islands (in the Atlantic Ocean), and two cities in North Africa: Ceuta and Melilla. Capital: Madrid Borders: France, Portugal, Andorra, and the Mediterranean Sea Landscape: Mountains (Pyrenees, Sierra Nevada), plains, rivers, and beaches


2. Government and Politics

Spain is a parliamentary constitutional monarchy. Monarch: King Felipe VI (as of 2025) Prime Minister: Leads the government (name may vary depending on current politics) It has 17 autonomous communities with their own governments


3. Language

Official Language: Spanish (Castilian) Other recognized regional languages: Catalan, Galician, Basque, and Aranese
